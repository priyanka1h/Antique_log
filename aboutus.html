About Us

Furniture is important part
for comfort

Antique Log is furniture design website that carries furniture design which is chosen carefully keeping in mind the different types of requirements of the customer.

Every product at Antique Log is curated with focus on quality and design. We intend to create spacious and uncluttered designs which matches with the current trend and also restore antique pieces.

The primary objective of our furniture design website is to provide a curated place for shopping furnitures with the best options for the customer.
General ecommerce web design rules and trends apply to furniture as well since its a targeted market of a specific genre.
The website will help in straight forward navigation, coherent visuals and feel good atmosphere for the customers to choose the best option available.

Some interesting facts are:
1. Online sales make up 10% of the total revenue and this number is slowly increasing.
2. Atleast 70% of the buyers search and browse products online first before making the purchase.
3.36% of the customers connect to the web while being in the physical store.
